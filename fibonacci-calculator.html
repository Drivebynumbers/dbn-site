What are Fibonacci Levels?
🎯
Retracement Levels
Price rarely moves in a straight line. Fibonacci retracements identify where price tends to pull back before continuing the trend. The 38.2%, 50%, and 61.8% zones are where most reversals occur.
✨
The Golden Zone (61.8%)
The 61.8% level — derived from the golden ratio — is the most respected level in technical analysis. Institutions accumulate or distribute heavily at this level. Combined with price action, it becomes a high-probability entry zone.
🚀
Extension Levels
Extensions (127.2%, 161.8%) project how far price might move beyond the swing high or low. Traders use these as take-profit targets after price breaks the 100% level and continues in the original direction.
